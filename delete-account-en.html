Introduction

Hello! Thank you for using MenuGreen — an app that helps you order food and manage your menu. If you want to delete your account and data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the MenuGreen app on your Android device.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  4. Optionally provide a reason and tap "Submit Deletion Request".
  5. Or email us directly with the subject "MenuGreen Account Deletion Request".
📧 Email your deletion request to: dinhtheduc407@gmail.com

Data That Will Be Permanently Deleted

  • Account information: display name, email, password (encrypted), avatar
  • Health information: personal preferences and settings
  • Meal data: meal history, menus, saved recipes
  • Favorites: saved foods, recipes, meal plans
  • Progress & stats: app usage history and saved preferences
  • Notifications: push notification history and FCM token

Data Retained (for legal/operational reasons)

  • Security logs: kept for 90 days for fraud prevention, then auto-deleted
  • Anonymized payment data: if any transaction occurred, retained per tax regulations
⚠️ Important: This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, all data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered. If you only want to take a break, consider logging out instead of deleting.
